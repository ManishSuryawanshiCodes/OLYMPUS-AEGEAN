Olympus Aegean Cafe – Greece Theme  
A Santorini-inspired cafe concept set in Goa, blending Olympus tradition with Aegean calm. The project highlights a clean, minimal white-and-blue design paired with handcrafted coffee and modern Mediterranean plates. Guests can enjoy Greek classics like freddo espresso, spanakopita, souvlaki wraps, and baklava, all prepared with fresh ingredients and thoughtful service.

Highlights:

🌿 Fresh Mediterranean flavors with olive oil, feta, herbs, and local produce

☕ Signature Greek coffee and brunch bowls

🏛️ Minimal ambience with serene interiors and warm lighting

🚚 Fast delivery across Panaji

⭐ Guest rating: 4.9/5 from 1200+ happy visitors

This project integrates food-first design with a timeless Greek cafe experience, offering both dine-in and delivery options in Fontainhas, Goa.
